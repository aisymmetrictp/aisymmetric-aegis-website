Privacy Policy

AISymmetric Aegis is a boutique security consultancy. We don't run analytics, don't sell data, and don't track visitors. This page is the short version of what that means in practice.

Last updated: 2026-04-19

What we collect

We only collect information you deliberately send us. There are exactly two places that happen on this site:

  • Assessment request form — name, email, company, role, company size, primary interest, timeline, and any free-text context you add.
  • Free Scan request form — name, email, optional company, and a brief note on what you plan to scan.

When you run a Quick Scan we also record the URL you submitted, your scan result summary (score, pass/warn/fail counts), timestamp, and the fingerprint of your 7-day access code. We keep this so the site owner can review tool usage and to deter abuse.

What we don't collect

  • No analytics, pixels, session replay, or behavioural tracking.
  • No advertising cookies, no remarketing lists.
  • No third-party scripts beyond Google Fonts (which serves the Inter typeface). Font files are cached by your browser; Google receives your IP as part of the request but we do not send them any additional data about you.

Cookies

We use essential storage only:

  • aegis_privacy_ack_v1 (localStorage) — records that you dismissed the privacy banner so it doesn't reappear.
  • aegis_access_code_v1 (localStorage, on /scan/ only) — stores your 7-day scanner access code so you don't have to paste it on every visit.

No tracking cookies. No third-party cookies. Clearing browser data removes both items.

How we use the information

  • To respond to your request and send the confirmation or access-code email.
  • To issue, verify, and expire Aegis Quick Scan access codes (7 days, stateless HMAC — we don't need to keep a copy).
  • Internal review of how the tool is being used. Scan logs are emailed to the site owner and retained in that inbox.

We do not use your information for marketing, profiling, or automated decisions. We do not sell or rent it. We do not share it with any party outside the sub-processors below.

Sub-processors

  • Cloudflare, Inc. — hosts the site (Cloudflare Pages) and handles TLS, CDN, and DDoS protection. Their processing of requests is governed by Cloudflare's privacy policy.
  • Resend, Inc. — transactional email delivery. When you submit a form, your email address and message content travel through Resend so we can send you a reply.

Data retention

  • Form submissions — kept in our inbox for as long as the conversation is active, then archived.
  • Scan logs — kept in our inbox for the site owner's reference; no separate database.
  • Access codes — never stored; validated statelessly and expire after 7 days.

You can request deletion at any time (see Contact below). We'll confirm within a reasonable timeframe.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port the information we hold about you, and to object to certain processing. Email us and we'll take care of it — we keep so little that most requests resolve in minutes.

Security

  • All traffic is served over HTTPS with HSTS.
  • Form submissions and access-code checks run on Cloudflare Pages Functions; the access-code secret is stored as a secret environment variable, not in the codebase.
  • We publish a vulnerability-reporting contact at /.well-known/security.txt.

Contact

Questions, complaints, or data requests: tyler.perleberg@aisymmetricsolutions.com.

We keep the policy short on purpose. If anything here is unclear, reply to any email from us and we'll rewrite the paragraph that confused you.

Changes to this policy

If this policy materially changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date and post the new version here. Previous versions are tracked in the site's public git history.
